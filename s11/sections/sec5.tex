      \end{align*}\vspace{-18pt}

        \item[SR2R:] R: Best response is to play $d$ as\\
          $\mathbb{E}[u_\text{R}(R,u|q$=$\frac{1}{2})]=1\frac{1}{2}+0\frac{1}{2}=\frac{1}{2}$\\
